## 3. Specific Component Requirements

### Resistors (Individual Values if not buying a kit)
* **1x 10 Ω** (0.5W Metal Film)
* **1x 56 Ω** (0.5W Metal Film)
* **1x 120 Ω** (0.5W Metal Film) - *Can be substituted with 110 Ω or 130 Ω depending on gauge calibration.*

### Consumables
* **Solder:** Rosin core (60/40 or Lead-Free).
* **Heat Shrink:** 3mm and 6mm diameters for wire protection.
* **Mounting:** 3M VHB tape or small nylon standoffs to secure the PCB inside the box.

---

### Pro-Tip for Ordering:
When ordering the **PC817 board**, ensure it features **screw terminals**. Some versions are sold as "bare pins" meant for breadboards; the screw terminal versions are much more reliable for high-vibration environments like boats or machinery.
